SYMBICORT Shout Out!

  • Form 2 teams [team A and Team B]
  • Wheel contains different categories
  • Spinning the wheel determines which category of questions will be asked
  • Question in selected category is presented
  • First team to signal by buzzing the buzzer gets the chance to respond
    • — Different team should signal each round
  • If answer is correct, team earns 1 point
  • If incorrect, other team gets the turn and chance to earn 1 point
  • Team with the most points wins SYMBICORT SHOUT OUT!
